What We Developed

This page highlights the modules and components developed during the creation of the AI Resume Analyzer system.

  • Resume Parser: Extracts structured data from resumes
  • Job Matcher: Matches resumes with job descriptions
  • Title Classifier: Predicts job titles using ML
  • Skill Extractor: Identifies direct and related skills
  • Scoring Engine: Ranks candidates based on multiple factors
