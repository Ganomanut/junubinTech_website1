AI Powered Resume Analyzer

An AI-based system that extracts structured information from resumes and predicts the best-fit job category and specialization using deep learning.

Home | What We Developed | Complete Project | Mission | Vision | Goals | Installation

Abstract

This AI-powered resume analyzer automatically parses resumes, extracts structured information, and evaluates how well each resume matches a job description. Built using TensorFlow and natural language processing, the system enhances recruitment efficiency through intelligent automation. Traditional keyword-based systems often fail to capture the nuanced alignment between candidate experience and job requirements. Our system addresses this by using a transformer-based NLP model to parse resumes, match them to job descriptions, and compute semantic and statistical similarity scores. It offers a unified backend for resume parsing and intelligent job matching that can integrate with job portals and support continuous learning.

Method Overview

The system parses unstructured resumes into structured formats and computes multi-factor similarity with job postings. It includes modules for skill extraction, job title prediction, and candidate ranking.

Key Features

  • Resume Parsing into structured JSON
  • Job Description Matching
  • Job Title Prediction using ML
  • Semantic Skill Extraction
  • Candidate Ranking and Score Visualization

Information Extracted

  • Job Title
  • Candidate Highlights
  • Direct Skill Matches
  • Related Skills
  • Experience Alignment
  • Keyword Frequency Score
  • Overall Matching Score
