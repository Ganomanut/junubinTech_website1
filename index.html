Abstract

This AI-powered resume analyzer automatically parses resumes, extracts structured information, and evaluates how well each resume matches a job description. Built using TensorFlow and natural language processing, the system enhances recruitment efficiency through intelligent automation.

Traditional keyword-based systems often fail to capture the nuanced alignment between candidate experience and job requirements. Our system addresses this by using a transformer-based NLP model to parse resumes, match them to job descriptions, and compute semantic and statistical similarity scores. It offers a unified backend for resume parsing and intelligent job matching that can integrate with job portals and support continuous learning.
