Abstract

The AI-Powered Resume Analyzer is a unified system for intelligent resume parsing and job matching. Traditional keyword-based systems often fail to understand contextual nuances and alignment between a candidate’s experience and job requirements. Our model leverages transformer-based NLP architecture to extract structured data, match resumes with job descriptions, and rank candidates based on semantic and statistical scores.

Method Overview

The system parses unstructured resumes into structured formats and computes multi-factor similarity with job postings. It includes modules for skill extraction, job title prediction, and candidate ranking.

Key Features

  • Resume Parsing into structured JSON
  • Job Description Matching
  • Job Title Prediction using ML
  • Semantic Skill Extraction
  • Candidate Ranking and Score Visualization
