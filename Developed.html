What We Developed

  • Resume Parser: Extracts structured information like job title, skills, experience, etc. from unstructured resumes.
  • Job Description Dataset: A comprehensive dataset including job titles, qualifications, salary range, and more.
  • Matching Algorithm: Computes similarity scores between resume and job description to assess candidate fit.
  • Visualization: Visualizes matching scores and candidate ranking.
  • Web Interface: User-friendly interface with multiple tabs for project info and installation guidance.
