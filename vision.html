Vision Statement

Our vision is to revolutionize hiring by making resume analysis faster, more accurate, and fair, leveraging artificial intelligence to support data-driven recruitment decisions.

About AI Powered Resume Analyzer

The AI Powered Resume Analyzer is an advanced system that extracts structured information from resumes and predicts the best-fit job category and specialization using deep learning. It leverages natural language processing and transformer-based models to parse resumes, match them to job descriptions, and compute semantic and statistical similarity scores. This solution enhances recruitment efficiency, reduces manual effort, and supports fair, data-driven hiring decisions.
